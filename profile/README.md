Overview
Built Different Apparel is a lifestyle and statement-driven apparel brand operating as a DBA under True North Data Strategies LLC.

This brand exists to represent resilience, grit, and earned strength. It's for people who didn't get here the easy way—and aren't interested in pretending otherwise.

No gimmicks. No manufactured motivation. Just presence.

Three Brand Voices, One Storefront
Built Different operates as the unified storefront for three distinct brand voices:

1. Built Different (Core Brand)
Focus: Mental health awareness, veteran resilience, suicide prevention
Audience: Veterans, first responders, survivors, anyone who's been through it
Voice: Raw. Honest. No motivational fluff.

Sample messaging:

Life tried me. I'm still here.
Still standing. Still fighting. Still here.
You're not broken. You're battle-tested.
The exit sign lied. There's more road.
2. TNDS Command Modules
Focus: Google Workspace automation, AI integration, operational systems
Audience: SMB owners, ops managers, people who want their business to run itself
Voice: Technical. Clean. Quietly confident.

Sample messaging:

Automate the boring. Focus on the mission.
If it's manual, it's a liability.
Scripts don't call in sick.
Your spreadsheet called. It wants a workflow.
3. Pipeline Punks
Focus: Technical education, DevOps culture, leadership with edge
Audience: Developers, IT pros, builders, and the people who teach them
Voice: Bold. Irreverent. Leadership meets punk rock.

Sample messaging:

Ship it or shut up.
Docs or it didn't happen.
The pipeline doesn't care about your feelings.
Lead from the terminal.
Merge conflicts build character.
